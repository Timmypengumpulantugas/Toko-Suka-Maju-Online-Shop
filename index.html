OUR PRODUCT

Mclaren Senna

$1 million USD

The McLaren Senna is a hypercar that embodies the pinnacle of McLaren’s engineering excellence, blending extreme performance with cutting-edge technology and an uncompromising focus on aerodynamics. Named after the legendary Formula 1 driver Ayrton Senna, this car is designed for those who demand unparalleled speed, precision, and driving excitement.

Apollo Intensa Emozione

$2.7 million usd

The Apollo Intensa Emozione (meaning “Intense Emotion” in Italian) is a breathtaking, limited-production hypercar created by German automaker Apollo Automobil in collaboration with Manifattura Automobili Torino. Unveiled in 2017, the IE is designed to deliver a raw, visceral driving experience that pays homage to the golden age of naturally aspirated engines.

Bugatti Turbillon

€3.8 million (approximately $4.1 million USD)

At the heart of the Tourbillon lies an 8.3-liter naturally aspirated V16 engine, delivering 1,000 horsepower. This is complemented by three electric motors—two at the front and one at the rear—producing an additional 800 horsepower, bringing the total system output to an impressive 1,800 horsepower. This hybrid powertrain enables the Tourbillon to accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in just 2.0 seconds, with a top speed exceeding 400 km/h, potentially reaching up to 445 km/h with the "unlimited" key

Porsche 911 GT3 RS

$244,995 USD

Under the hood, the 911 GT3 RS is powered by a 4.0-liter flat-six engine, producing 518 horsepower at 8,500 rpm and 342 lb-ft of torque at 6,300 rpm. This engine is paired with a seven-speed automatic transmission, enabling the car to accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in approximately 2.7 seconds, with a top speed around 310 km/h.

Mirov II LIMITED

$1 million USD

The Mirov II is a unique, one-off sports car that originated as a fictional Soviet supercar in a 1989 British television advertisement for Norwich Union insurance. Designed by English props designer Jon Bunker, the car was intended to showcase a futuristic Russian vehicle. Two full-scale mock-ups were created: one as a non-functional prop and another powered by a VW Beetle engine .​

AMG G 63

$475,000 USD

The Mercedes-Benz G-Class (G-Wagon) is a luxury SUV known for its rugged off-road ability, bold boxy design, and high-end comfort. It blends iconic military styling with modern tech and powerful engines, including V8 and hybrid options.
